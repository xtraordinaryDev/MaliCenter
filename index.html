We Believe

At the Mali Center, we know that all people have immeasurable value and can reach their potential with coaching & development, mentorship and inspiration to fuel their growth. All individuals deserve equitable resources and investment.

Who We Are

The Mali Center is a health and wealth business hub that embraces diversity in an inclusive environment while providing equitable access and opportunity to underrepresented businesses as well as nonprofits that prioritize underestimated youth and communities of color.

Not just a physical space, The Mali Center is an ecosystem of entrepreneurs, a community unifier and a space that prioritizes holistic health and wellness. We provide a culturally responsible environment and give our community members the support they need to scale their businesses and serve their communities.

📈

🧠

Managing mood and challenges while moderating trauma and remaining hopeful.

✨

Feeling connected to self, others, and all of humanity.

🤝

Social

Having an ecosystem of supportive, positive people and maintaining vital relationships.
